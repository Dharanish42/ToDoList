About This To-Do List Application

This simple and efficient To-Do List application allows you to manage your tasks effectively. Whether you’re planning your day, organizing your work, or just jotting down things you don’t want to forget, this app helps you stay on top of everything.

Features

  • Add, complete, and delete tasks with ease.
  • Simple and intuitive user interface.
  • Responsive design, so it works well on both desktop and mobile devices.

How to Use

To add a task, simply type in the task you want to add and click the "Add" button. You can mark a task as complete by clicking the "Complete" button, or remove it from your list by clicking "Delete". The app is designed to be user-friendly and requires no learning curve.
